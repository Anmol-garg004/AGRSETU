AGRSETU is a high-end agricultural fintech platform designed to bridge the gap between rural farmers and institutional credit. By leveraging satellite imagery, AI-driven credit scoring, and a personalized digital interface, we empower farmers to unlock their financial potential while providing lenders with verified, low-risk data.
